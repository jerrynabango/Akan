Introduction

The Akan is the largest ethnic group in Ghana which comprises of many clans,who speak the Twi-Fante language and also found in some parts of Cote d'Ivore. The Akan names are derived from the Ghanian Culture.In Ghana children are usually given their first names as 'day name' which actually corresponds to the day of the week they were born. Most of the people do know when their birthday's are,but some might not know what day of the week they were born. Luckily for us,there are ways to calculate the day of the week from specific date.
